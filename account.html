Retirement
Roth IRA

A Roth IRA allows contributions and potential investment gains to accumulate tax-free. Withdrawals can be taken out tax-free when they qualify and there are no mandatory withdrawals (unlike a Traditional IRA).

Traditional IRA

A traditional IRA allows you to increase your tax-deferred retirement savings if you expect to be in the same or lower tax bracket. There are no income limitations to open an account. And current year contributions may be tax-deductible.

Rollover IRA

If you are looking to move funds from an old employer-sponsored retirement plan, consider a Rollover Individual Retirement Account (IRA). Our rollover specialists can help you from start to finish.

Note: A rollover of retirement plan assets to an IRA is not your only option. Carefully consider all of your available options, which may include but not be limited to keeping your assets in your former employer's plan, rolling over assets to a new employer's plan, or taking a cash distribution (taxes and possible withdrawal penalties may apply). Prior to a decision, be sure to understand the benefits and limitations of your available options and consider factors such as differences in investment-related expenses, plan or account fees, available investment options, distribution options, legal and creditor protections, the availability of loan provisions, tax treatment, and other concerns specific to your individual circumstances.
Inherited IRA

Transfer your inherited IRA or employee-sponsored retirement plan account to a APTIV Inherited IRA and get one-on-one help and guidance. All the tax advantages of an IRA plus the ability to withdraw funds according to a set schedule over your lifetime.

Custodial IRA

If you're the parent of a child who has earned income, a Custodial IRA can help teach the value of saving and investing. Besides getting a head start on saving, your child may be able to use the funds for college expenses—or even to buy a first home.

Personal Choice Retirement Account

This is a self-directed brokerage account that resides within an employer-sponsored retirement plan. In addition to the core investment options offered by a retirement plan, PCRA provides premium research and tools and the choice to invest in a much wider range of investments.

Small Business Retirement
Individual 401(k) Plan

Retirement plan for owner-only businesses with no employees. Works like a 401(k). Has high contribution limits and is easy to administer.

SEP-IRA

Retirement plan for owner-only businesses or those with just a few employees. There's little administration, tax filing isn't required, and you can vary contributions from year to year—or even skip a year.

SIMPLE IRA

Retirement plan for businesses with up to 100 employees. Primarily funded with employee salary-deferral contributions. Employer-matched contributions up to 3%. An easy and economical plan to administer

Personal Defined Benefit Plan

Retirement plan for owner-only businesses or those with up to five employees. Allows very high contributions, which are generally 100% tax-deductible.

Business 401(k) Plan

Retirement plan for businesses of any size. Permits a higher level of salary deferrals by employees than other plans. Optional annual employer contributions.

Annuities
Variable Annuities

Unlimited tax-deferred growth potential. The optional living benefit rider can provide guaranteed* income (additional cost), and the optional death benefit (additional cost) can help pass on money to heirs.

Fixed Indexed Annuities

Principal protection with the potential for limited tax-deferred growth. The optional living benefit rider can provide a 'retirement paycheck' for life (additional cost).

Single Premium Immediate Annuities

Single Premium Immediate Annuities allow you to use a lump sum of your assets to purchase a 'retirement paycheck' for life. As the contracts are irrevocable, the income amounts are generally higher than those provided by income options available with variable and/or fixed indexed annuities.

Deferred Income Annuities

Deferred Income Annuities allow you to use a lump sum or multiple purchases to receive a 'retirement paycheck' for life, starting at a future date of your choosing. Generally, the longer you defer taking income, the greater your 'retirement paycheck' will be.

Fixed Deferred Annuities

Principal protection with a guaranteed* rate of return (free from market volatility). In exchange for limited liquidity, Fixed Deferred Annuities offer attractive rates compared to other alternatives.

*All guarantees depend on the claims-paying ability and financial strength of the issuing insurance company, not APTIV Bank.
