Governance

Governance

The APTIV Bank Executive Committee’s mission includes bringing together both state of the art and diverse expertise and collegiality in the decision-making process of APTIV Bank at top level, supporting the CEO. In accordance with French law, APTIV Bank Holding’s CEO reports to the APTIV Bank Holding board of directors (“Board”), who oversees in particular, APTIV Bank’s global strategy, risks, and control systems.

The Executive Committee advises the CEO on decisions and business matters of the global asset management business line, spanning business management, strategy implementation, and definition and enforcement of policies. The governance in place is strong and coherent both from a local and global perspective. It is well-suited for an asset management business part of a banking group.

Let it be emphasized that APTIV Bank Holding’s Board chose to dissociate the roles of Chair of the Board and CEO, as one of its key missions under French law is to foster balance and accountability at the highest level of APTIV Bank’s organization.
